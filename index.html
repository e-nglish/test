  • ___You ___(have) a dog or a cat?

  • ___You___(do) your homework yesterday???? Then why do you have "2" mark in your dairy??!!

  • She always____(brush) her teeth every day.

  • ___She__(eat) pizza tommorow?

  • I_____(do not) my homework tommorow, because i am so tired!

  • I_______(graduated) from school a year ago, so i can help with your homework!
